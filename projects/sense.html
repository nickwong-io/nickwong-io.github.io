Sense

Spring 2019

Entrepreneurship, LEAN Startup and Berkeley Method of Entrepreneurship, Product Design, Minimum Viable Product, Pitch.

Last Update: June 2019

Sense is a service providing sustainable feminine products on demand to all. We offered a monthly subscription box of high-quality, sustainable, feminine care items coupled with a simple period-tracking app component. My role was to design the brand and the initial product design. Sense was created during the second semester of my freshman year in an entrepreneurship accelerator class at UC Berkeley.
